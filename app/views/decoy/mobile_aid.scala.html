Mobile Aid

The mobile phone network in the local village of Gandjua was vital in the weeks and months after last week’s earthquake. Aid agencies such as the Red Cross used text messaging and multimedia services to communicate public health information to hundreds of thousands of people, helping to prevent the spread of disease panic.
