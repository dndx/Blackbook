Human Rights

The The Caring Donations Community project is working hard to guarantee that all citizens of this world have their rights protected.  In our most recent venture, we worked to help educate the children of Albanter.  The world starts with our children.

Clean Water

In Sinbahna, a new initiative is transforming the lives of local villagers by providing access to free, safe water.  The Human Aid International project has installed hundreds of viable drinking wells and trained the locals on safe water habits.

Mobile Aid

The mobile phone network in the local village of Gandjua was vital in the weeks and months after last week’s earthquake. Aid agencies such as the Red Cross used text messaging and multimedia services to communicate public health information to hundreds of thousands of people, helping to prevent the spread of disease panic.
